## Requirements

### Concave Wells

very nice, must have.

### Layout

3 rows, with 6 keys each suffices, but preferable 4 rows in case at some point
in the future i wanna go back to standard qwerty or qwertz.

### Thumb Clusters

Each cluster has to have at least 2 buttons (tho i currently use 3, i plan to move down to 2 in expectation it is good enough).
The thumb cluster buttons have to be positioned next to the rest of the keys, not beneath them, thats very terrible.

### Portability

as long as you can wrap them into some soft cloth and put em in a back pack it should be fine.

### Programmability

programmable, I need layers and homerow mods.
I should be programmable with QMK/ZMK without additional
firmware. (Glove80 has its own firmware ontop of ZMK,
meaning you loose functionality if you dont use it. Disgusting.)

### Connectivity

bluetooth optional, I love wireless, but I think i prefer wired in the future.
Issues being, cant select boot menu things or even bios without bluetooth.
Cant sync bluetooth devices across dual boot without extra effort, didnt do it,
too much effort (also wasnt important enough to be able to use my glove80 on windows).

QMK=wired, more features afaik (but no bluetooth support)
ZMK=not as much features, but supports bluetooth

> if it is a bluetooth keyboard it reeeaally needs to connect fast. 
> My softle choc is terribly slow to connect, bugs out and comes with idle/sleep functionallity.
> Starting to hate bluetooth thinking of all those problems which could be solved with a wire.

### Split Design

two physical splits, are a must have for ergo.

### Acoustics

as quiet as possible, with the least amount of punctiation force 
(i believe that is what it was called).
I think the Glove80 cherry switches are unbeatable in combination to the 
small light keycaps. But Ill have to settle with less, I guess.

### Hot swapable

not sure, I think it is preferable. Dont have too much experience with other switches,
would be a shame if i werent able to swap em, without soldering.

### Ortholinear/Columnar

must have
